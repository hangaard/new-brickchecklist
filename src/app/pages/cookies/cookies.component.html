Privacy and Cookies Policy

Privacy Policy

For the moment we store user data only on the user's own device or computer.

We use Google Analytics to register events when the home page is visited and when new checklists are created. Also the approximate location of the users are registered with Google.
We do this to be able to study the demographics of our users and also to learn from how users are using our application, so that we can get ideas as of how to improve it.

Cookies Policy

All checklist data is stored on the user's device or computer in what is called local storage.

The checklists are stored locally for better performance.

We do plan to also store the checklists in the cloud to enable working on the checklists on different devices/computers.
