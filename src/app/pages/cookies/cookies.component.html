Privacy and Cookies Policy

Privacy Policy

For the moment we store user data only on the user's own device or computer.

We use Google Analytics to register events when the home page is visited and when new checklists are created. Also the approximate location (country and city) of the users are registered with Google.
We do this to be able to see where in the world the application is being used and also to learn from how users are using our application, so that we can get ideas for improvemens.

Cookies Policy

All checklist data is stored on the user's device or computer in what is called local storage.

The checklists are stored locally for better performance.

We do plan to also store the checklists in the cloud to enable working on the checklists on different devices/computers.
